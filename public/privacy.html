Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Sacred Healing ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our app and services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, name, and profile picture (if provided).

Usage Data: We collect usage information such as meditation sessions completed, streaks, progress, achievements, and SHC token balance.

Content: If you use our Creative Soul meditation tool, we may process audio you create or upload for the purpose of providing the service.

Payment Information: For subscriptions and purchases, payment processing is handled by third-party providers. We do not store full payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our meditation, healing, and wellness services
  • Track your progress, streaks, and rewards (SHC tokens)
  • Process subscriptions and purchases
  • Send you updates and support (with your consent)
  • Improve our app and user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., hosting, analytics) who assist in operating our app
  • Payment processors for subscription and purchase transactions
  • Legal authorities when required by law

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure authentication.

5. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account data through the app settings. You may also request a copy of your data or withdraw consent for certain processing.

6. Children

Our app is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 without parental consent.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via the app or email.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at: sacredhealingvibe@gmail.com
