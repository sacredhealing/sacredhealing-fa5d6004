Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026  ·  Effective immediately
Your privacy matters deeply to us. This policy explains exactly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We never sell your data.

This Privacy Policy applies to the Sacred Healing platform operated by Siddha Quantum Nexus ("we," "us," or "our"), accessible at sacredhealing.lovable.app.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and password (stored encrypted via Supabase authentication).

Profile Information: Optionally, your birth date and location (used for Vedic astrology features), preferred language, and membership tier.

Usage Data: Which meditations, audios, and features you access, your session duration, and interaction patterns within the app.

Payment Information: Processed entirely by Stripe. We do not store your card details. We only receive confirmation of successful payments.

Social Media Tokens: If you connect your social media accounts (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Facebook) to use the content publishing feature, we store OAuth access tokens in encrypted form. These are used only to post content on your behalf when you explicitly initiate a post.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize your Sacred Healing experience
  • To process membership payments and manage your subscription
  • To send weekly alignment emails and important account notifications
  • To power AI features (Vedic astrology, Ayurveda AI, Quantum Apothecary) with your birth chart and profile data
  • To publish content to your social media accounts when you use the Content Publisher tool
  • To improve platform features based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

3. Social Media Integration

When you connect your social media accounts through our Content Publisher tool:

  • TikTok: We use the TikTok Content Posting API solely to upload and publish videos to your TikTok account when you initiate a post. We access your basic profile info (username, display name) to verify the connection. We do not read your TikTok feed, followers, or messages.
  • Instagram & Facebook: We use Meta's Graph API to publish posts and Reels to your connected pages and profiles. We do not access your private messages or follower data.
  • YouTube: We use the YouTube Data API to upload videos to your channel. We do not access your subscribers, analytics, or private data.

You can revoke these connections at any time from your account settings or directly from each platform's settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted in the EU. We use row-level security, encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), and encrypted storage for sensitive tokens. We regularly review security practices to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Stripe — for payment processing
  • Supabase — for database and authentication
  • Resend — for transactional email delivery
  • Anthropic / Google — AI features send anonymized prompts to generate responses. No personal identifiers are sent.
  • Social platforms — only the content you explicitly choose to publish

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU/EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Opt out of any non-essential data processing

To exercise any of these rights, email sacredhealingvibe@gmail.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use minimal, essential cookies for authentication (keeping you logged in) and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Sacred Healing is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us immediately.

9. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we permanently remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law to retain it longer.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify active members by email of any significant changes. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance.
